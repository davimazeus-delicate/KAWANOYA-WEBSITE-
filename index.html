Our Story · 川の屋について

Where Japan Meets
San Francisco Soul

Kawanoya — meaning "house by the river" — was born from a singular vision: to bring the meditative, deeply nourishing cuisine of Japan to the heart of San Francisco. Every dish we serve is a quiet ceremony, a pause from the pace of the city.

We source only the finest ingredients — ocean-fresh salmon, heritage-breed duck, single-origin Japanese rice, and hand-selected seasonal vegetables — because authenticity cannot be faked, only honored.
